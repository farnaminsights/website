SAT/ACT

Our tutors are Yale students who scored in the 99th percentile of the category they are teaching in. We guide students to the resources they need and take the time to answer and clarify any questions they may have while preparing for one of the most important exams of their life. The SAT and ACT are two different tests, and some people are better suited for one than the other. We help students figure out early on which test they should take and build an effective testing timeline to allow students to achieve the highest score they can by the time they submit scores to colleges.

SAT Subject Tests

Although SAT Subject Tests are optional for most colleges, they are essentially an additional requirement to gain admission into elite colleges. Most colleges want 2-3 subject tests, and depending on the school as well as the student’s prospective major, students will need to have different combinations of these tests. We work with students to decide which tests will be the best for them to take and help students prepare for them.

PSAT

Getting a top PSAT score will qualify you for National Merit consideration which is a route for scholarship opportunities and preferred admission in many colleges. While it is not an official SAT score, it is a good indicator of a student’s future SAT score range. Many universities offer extensive scholarships (some even offer full-ride scholarships) to students who become National Merit Finalists. Through our PSAT tutoring program, our younger clients become more comfortable with the SAT and will have a better sense of what to expect during the college application process.
